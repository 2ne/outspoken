How it works.

1. Looking for the right voice?

Whether you know exactly what type of voice you are looking for or still open-minded, OutSpoken can help. You can browse our extensive voice database; just click to hear their audio sample. Alternatively, post your FREE casting. Let our voice over actors know what your project involves and any requirements you have. Those that fit your brief will send you a free casting voice over sample.

2. You've Found The Voice!

After browsing or utilising a free open casting, you’ve found the perfect voice for your next project. Congratulations! Now all you need to do is confirm the details (the script, timings, style) and click ‘Hire’.

3. Receive Your High-Quality Audio

After clicking hire and inputting your payment details, your artist will then get to work creating your masterpiece. You will be put in direct contact with the selected talent, so you can discuss the fine details or arrange a dial in to the artist’s studio to direct the session. OutSpoken will notify you, when your recording is ready to be downloaded.
